About Us

Gravity Associates Inc. provides business and technology consulting services to small, medium and large financial services companies. Our business model provides consultants on a project basis with extensive experience. The average experience of our team is near 20 years with no staff with less than 10 years. This allows Gravity Associates to provide grounded experience based on past projects successes at rates well below the large consulting firms.

Leadship

John Kelly is Founder and President of Gravity Associates Inc.
Kelly has 25 plus years of experience in Financial Services spanning Finance, Risk and Technology. Before founding Gravity, Kelly was at LaSalle Bank in Chicago focused Financial Systems and Reporting and later moved to Technology as Divisional CIO for the Retail bank and Shared Services.
Kelly enjoys endurance sports including Triathlon and Running as well as outdoor sports such as Mountaineering. Rock Climbing and Backcountry Hiking.
Kelly graduated from University of Illinois at Chicago and received his MBA from University of Chicago. He also completed his certified public accountant exam.
