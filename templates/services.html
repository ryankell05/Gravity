Gravity Associates Service Offerings

Comprehensive Capital Adequacy Reporting (CCAR) Service Offerings

The complex and ever increasing CCAR requirements and expectations of the bank regulators forces CCAR efforts to the forefront on many financial services institutions priority lists due to MRA’s, MRIA’s or worse failure of consent to an institution’s Capital Plan. Understanding the direction of peer banks in this regulatory environment is critical to stay with the pack and therefore ahead of the regulators expectations. Gravity Associates provides services supporting:

  • Data acquisition for FR Y-14 M&Q reporting
  • Data acquisition for Credit Loss, PPNR and Balance Sheet Modeling
  • Basel 239 Risk Data Aggregation governance, reporting and validation
  • Capital Plan aggregation, challenge and documentation processes and tools
  • Acquisition readiness and integration
